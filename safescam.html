What is a protect yourself from scam

An internet scam is a form of fraud conducted over the internet. It can take various forms, but generally involves deceiving people into providing personal information, money, or other valuable resources using fake websites, emails, messages, or social media. This can include fake online stores, phishing emails pretending to be from reputable companies, false investment opportunities, or even romantic scams where someone pretends to be interested in a relationship to obtain money. The essence lies in deceiving individuals or organizations to gain financial profit or acquire sensitive information that can be misused.
